Why Choose Aluzinc?

At the heart of every Colourful Roof tile is Aluzinc —a high-performance alloy of Aluminum, Zinc, and Silicon. This core provides the backbone for our lifetime guarantee.

  • check_circle
    Superior Corrosion Resistance The Aluzinc coating protects the steel core up to 6 times longer than standard galvanized steel, even in coastal environments.
  • check_circle
    Lightweight Structural Safety 7x lighter than concrete tiles, significantly reducing the structural load on your home and improving earthquake safety.
  • check_circle
    Advanced Heat Insulation The reflective properties of Aluzinc combined with our stone coating keep your home cooler in summer and warmer in winter.
