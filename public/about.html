Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about our premium roofing solutions and installation process.

What is Aluzinc? expand_more
Aluzinc is a specialized coating consisting of 55% Aluminum, 43.4% Zinc, and 1.6% Silicon. This unique combination provides exceptional corrosion resistance, often lasting 2-4 times longer than standard galvanized steel in similar environments.
How long do stone-coated tiles last? expand_more
Our stone-coated steel tiles are engineered for longevity. With proper installation, these roofs are expected to last between 50 to 70 years, weathering extreme conditions including high winds, hail, and heavy UV exposure.
Is there a warranty? expand_more
Yes. Every Colourful Roof installation comes with a comprehensive manufacturer's warranty covering the base metal and stone coating for 50 years.
